# VoiStock

VoiStock is a voice database which allows users to search for their favourite voice and listen to it.

## Connect the World with Voice and Technology

1. Digital Data Stock & Distribution Service
2. Conditional Voice & Video Distribution System
3. Immersive Digital Stamp Rally Service
4. Digital Quiz System with Digital Rewards
5. Simple and High-Quality AR Camera System
6. Customized Service Solutions

### Digital Data Stock & Distribution Service

This service allows for the storage and management of voice, video, and other digital data by adding various tags and information to the database. We support the development of revenue-generating models based on usage frequency, playback time, and other metrics. The data is distributed in formats optimized for the user’s device and environment.

### Conditional Voice & Video Distribution System

This system enables highly customizable conditions for distributing voice and video content. Depending on the client’s service, it allows for flexible settings on what content is distributed, to whom, and in what manner. Distribution can be customized based on location data, QR codes, playback history of other voice content, and more.

### Immersive Digital Stamp Rally Service

We offer an immersive stamp rally service utilizing voice and video. It allows the distribution of location-specific voice and video content. Users can enjoy exclusive content tied to each spot, and complete the rally to unlock additional GPS-linked voice content, enhancing the immersive experience.

### Digital Quiz System with Digital Rewards

This customizable quiz system offers exclusive voice and video content based on scores and participation. Users can learn through quizzes while experiencing simulated events with voice and video content. The system also enables the issuance of digital certificates with scores, participation dates, and even voice-embedded wallpapers.

### Simple and High-Quality AR Camera System

Our AR camera system can be customized with logos, characters, and frames. Similar to our voice distribution services, restrictions and conditions for AR usage can be set according to the client's needs.

### Customized Service Solutions

We offer tailored solutions that combine voice distribution, video distribution, quiz systems, stamp rally systems, and AR systems to create new user experiences and build innovative services based on specific client needs.​​
